## Titik Kasir

Cashier Point is a SaaS application for small shop cashier systems. The goal is to enable them to have a better sales recording system without incurring significant costs.

## Feature

- POS (Point of Sale)
- Product Management
- Category Management
- Transaction Management
- Report Management
